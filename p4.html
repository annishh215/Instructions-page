. Rules and Regulations :
Not to carry:
  • Compressed gases - deeply refrigerated, flammable, non-flammable and poisonous such as butane oxygen, liquid nitrogen, aqualung cylinders and compressed gas cylinders
  • Corrosives such as acids:
    1. alkalis
    2. mercury and wet cell batteries
    3. apparatus containing mercury
  • Explosives, munitions, fireworks and flares, ammunition including blank cartridges, handguns, fire works, pistol caps
  • Flammable liquids and solids such as:
    1. lighter refills
    2. lighter fuel
    3. matches
    4. paints
    5. thinners
    6. fire-lighters
    7. lighters that need inverting before ignition
    8. matches (these may be carried on the person)
    9. radioactive material
    10. briefcases and attache case with installed alarm devices
  • Oxidizing materials such as bleaching powder and peroxides
  • Poisons and infectious substances such as:
    1. insecticides
    2. weed-killers
    3. live virus materials
  • Dry cell batteries
  • Knives, scissors, Swiss army knives and other sharp instruments
  • Electronic devices which cannot be switched off
  • Aerosols and liquids
Do not :
  • Pull the chain unecessarily, in which case there will be a fine Rs.10000 and imprisonment of 10 years
  • Tamper with the seats and electronics on the coach
  • Litter the washrooms and walkway
